To turn these PNG sets into skyspheres for Unreal follow this tutorial video, it is about 20 minutes long but works perfectly
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l3sN0ZQDEpY
Make sure that the images line up on the cube before turbo smooth
Make sure that the poly's are two sided in Unreal
Make sure that Unreal uses the textures as emmisive as well as the normal, because the skyshpere wont be lit in the scene, so this will light itself.
